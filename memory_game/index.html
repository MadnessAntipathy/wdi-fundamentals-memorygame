Memory Game

Think you have a good memory?

Lots of people claim they have a good working memory. Your challenge, if you choose to accept is to see how many levels you can complete in this game.

About the game

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The objective of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
